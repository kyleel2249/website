Why scores disagree

Lighthouse (lab) vs. CrUX (field)

Lighthouse
Lab data
  • Single simulated page load, one machine
  • Useful for catching regressions pre-launch
  • Not what Google uses to rank pages
  • Can show 100 while CrUX shows a fail
VS
CrUX
Field data
  • Real visitors, real devices, real networks
  • Rolling 28-day average
  • This is the actual ranking signal
  • Fixes take ~4 weeks to fully reflect
